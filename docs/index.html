About Riley

Riley helps children notice, name and manage feelings using the Zones of Regulation framework. Riley is a practice buddy, not a therapist. If feelings stay big or heavy, talk to a trusted adult.

Works in any modern browser. On a VR headset (like Meta Quest), open this page in the headset’s browser and press “Enter VR” or “Enter AR”.

🔒 Privacy: check-ins and the journal stay in your browser — no accounts, no sign-ups, nothing stored anywhere else. Riley’s voice uses Microsoft’s online neural voices, so only the words Riley speaks out loud are sent to that service to make the audio. If you type or talk to Riley, that message is sent to Pollinations.AI (a free service, no account needed) to create Riley’s reply, and the microphone uses your browser’s built-in speech recognition — you can turn free chat off above. If a service can’t be reached, Riley falls back to the built-in voice and buttons.
